Oatmeal Recipe

Description

A very good food choice in terms of health, and just a delicious meal!

Ingredients

  • Oatmeal
  • Milk
  • Salt
  • Honey
  • Butter

Instructions

  1. Pour 1 part of milk in a pan, and just about bring it to a boil
  2. Pour in 1 part of the cereal, while simultaneously stirring the milk
  3. Add a pinch of salt
  4. Keep stirring the mix for about 7-10 minutes
  5. When the oatmeal reaches the desired viscosity, turn off the oven
  6. Cover the pan with a lid, for around 2-3 minutes
  7. Add butter and honey, and mix until smooth
