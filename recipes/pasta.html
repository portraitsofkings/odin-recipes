Pasta Recipe

Description

Macaroni!

Ingredients

  • Pasta
  • Butter

Instructions

  1. Pour around 2 parts of water into a pot
  2. Bring it to a boil
  3. Add salt and taste the water, until it feels just about right
  4. Add pasta, and right after, stir it a bit, so that it doesn't stick
  5. Let it cook on a medium heat, and either wait 7-10 minutes, or refer to instructions on the packaging
  6. When done, drain the water in a colander, and don't rinse afterwards
  7. You may as well pour back into the pot, or straight in a bowl (if there isn't too many)
  8. Add butter, and bon appetit!
